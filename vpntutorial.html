VPN Tutorial

Learn what a VPN is and how to use it to keep your internet safe and private.

What is a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a tool that helps keep your internet activity private. It hides where you are and protects what you do online.

Think of it like a private tunnel for your internet — no one else can see what you’re doing while you’re connected.

Learn More

Why Use a VPN?

  • Keeps your data safe when using public Wi-Fi
  • Hides your location from websites
  • Lets you see content from other countries
  • Adds extra privacy and safety online

VPNs are useful for anyone who wants a safer and more private internet experience.
